## 🧾 About the Project

**Yatra India** is a full-featured travel and tourism website that helps users discover, plan, and book unforgettable travel experiences across India. The platform is built around three core values:

- 🗺️ **Seamless Travel Planning** — handles every aspect of the journey, from accommodations to activities
- ✨ **Tailored Experiences** — personalized itineraries and expert guidance for every traveller
- 🤝 **Reliable & Trustworthy** — commitment to quality service, integrity, and professionalism

*"Let's Embark On Your Dream Journey — Discover Inspiring Destinations, Create Unforgettable Memories, And Travel With Confidence."*

---

## 🖥️ Pages & Features

- 🏠 **Home Page** — Hero section with search bar, core value highlights, and navigation to all sections
- 🗺️ **Destinations** — City Escapes & Nature Retreats featuring Banaras, India Gate, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, and Jaipur
- 🧳 **Travel Packages** — Curated multi-day tour packages across India's top destinations
- 🏨 **Exclusive Hotel Deals** — Featured offers with real hotel listings, ratings, and per-day pricing
- 📍 **Recently Visited Locations** — Highlights of Goa, Bengaluru, and Jammu Kashmir
- 🚗 **Transport Options** — Choose from Bike, Bus, Bicycle, Cab, and Flights
- ⭐ **Traveller Reviews** — Real reviews from travellers with experiences and ratings
- 📰 **Latest Articles** — Resources section with travel blogs on family travel, budget tips, food & travel, and destination discovery
- 💼 **Careers** — Open roles for Travel Consultants, Tour Guides, and Marketing Specialists
- 🔐 **Login & Sign Up** — User authentication for a personalised experience
- 📬 **Book Tickets** — Quick ticket booking form with location selector in the footer

## 📖 Usage

1. Open the website and land on the **Home Page**
2. Use the **search bar** to find destinations or packages
3. Browse **Destinations** to explore cities and nature retreats across India
4. Check **Travel Packages** for curated tour options
5. View **Exclusive Deals** for discounted hotel listings with ratings and pricing
6. Choose your preferred **Transport** — Bike, Bus, Cab, Flights, or Bicycle
7. Read **Explorer Articles** for travel tips, budget guides, and destination discovery
8. **Sign Up / Login** to personalise your experience
9. Use the **Book Tickets** form in the footer to quickly reserve your spot
